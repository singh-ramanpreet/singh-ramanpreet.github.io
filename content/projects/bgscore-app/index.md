BGScore is a frictionless companion for board game enthusiasts, designed to streamline session logging and elevate the gaming experience through data-driven insights. 

The application supports various scoring methods including category, round, and team-based systems while also offering a unique AI-powered recap feature that transforms raw statistics into engaging, shareable narratives.
